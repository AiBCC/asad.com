About Me

My Journey

My journey started with a passion for problem-solving and understanding how things work beneath the surface. This curiosity naturally led me toward data, where I discovered the perfect blend of logic, creativity, and real-world impact.

I pursued my BS in Information Technology from Bahauddin Zakariya University (BZU), which gave me a strong foundation in programming, databases, and software concepts. To further deepen my expertise, I completed the NAVTTC Onsite AI Bootcamp, where I gained hands-on experience in machine learning, data analysis, and modern AI tools.

Along the way, I began learning Python, SQL, and Power BI—starting with basic data exploration and gradually moving into building dashboards, writing optimized queries, and applying machine learning techniques. Each project I completed strengthened my belief that data is more than numbers; it’s a powerful tool for answering questions, improving decisions, and creating meaningful change.
